Why register a .com domain name?

With over 141 million registrations, the .com domain is the most popular and trusted extension in the world. It's the top choice for anyone building a new website because it helps establish your business and build trust with your customers.

It's also the most assumed extension - people naturally type it in if they don't know the website extension they're searching for.

Likewise, if an extension is not specified in the search bar, many web browsers will automatically find .com domains first. So, by simply registering a .com domain name, you can benefit from 'type-in traffic' too.
